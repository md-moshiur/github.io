Surface Treatment Technologies

Advanced surface modification techniques for enhanced performance and durability

Plasma Treatments

Ion bombardment and plasma-enhanced diffusion processes for surface modification.

  • • Plasma nitriding
  • • Plasma carburizing
  • • Ion implantation
  • • Plasma cleaning

PVD Coatings

Physical vapor deposition of thin, hard coatings for extreme performance.

  • • TiN (Titanium Nitride)
  • • TiAlN (Titanium Aluminum Nitride)
  • • CrN (Chromium Nitride)
  • • Diamond-like carbon (DLC)

CVD Processes

Chemical vapor deposition for thick, uniform coatings at high temperatures.

  • • TiC (Titanium Carbide)
  • • Al₂O₃ (Aluminum Oxide)
  • • SiC (Silicon Carbide)
  • • Multilayer coatings

Thermal Spray

High-velocity coating application for thick, protective layers.

  • • HVOF (High Velocity Oxy-Fuel)
  • • Plasma spray
  • • Arc spray
  • • Cold spray

Laser Treatments

Laser-based surface modification for precise control and localized treatment.

  • • Laser hardening
  • • Laser alloying
  • • Laser cladding
  • • Laser peening

Diffusion Treatments

Advanced thermochemical diffusion processes for enhanced surface properties.

  • • Boronizing
  • • Chromizing
  • • Siliconizing
  • • Vanadizing
